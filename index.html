Our dental services

Comprehensive care for every stage of your smile.

From routine checkups to root canals and oral surgery, Dental Zone Mianwali covers preventive, cosmetic, restorative, and surgical needs — for patients of all ages.

General Dentistry

Your first line of defense against cavities and gum problems. Regular checkups let Dr. Hanif catch issues early — before they turn into pain or expensive procedures.

  • Dental Checkups
  • Dental Consultation
  • Oral Examination
  • Preventive Care

Cosmetic Dentistry

For patients who want their smile to look as healthy as it feels. We plan whitening and smile-design cases around your natural face shape, not a one-size template.

  • Teeth Whitening
  • Smile Designing
  • Composite Bonding
  • Cosmetic Fillings

Restorative Dentistry

Rebuilding teeth that have been damaged by decay, wear, or injury — using tooth-colored materials so the repair blends in and function is fully restored.

  • Tooth-Colored Fillings
  • Dental Crowns
  • Dental Bridges
  • Dentures

Root Canal Treatment

When decay or infection reaches the nerve, root canal therapy saves the natural tooth instead of extracting it. Dr. Hanif specializes in comfortable single-visit procedures wherever possible.

  • Single Visit Root Canal
  • Multi Visit Root Canal
  • Tooth Restoration

Oral Surgery

From a straightforward extraction to an impacted wisdom tooth, surgical procedures are performed with sterilized instruments and careful attention to a smooth recovery.

  • Tooth Extraction
  • Wisdom Tooth Removal
  • Minor Oral Surgical Procedures

Children's Dentistry

Gentle, patient care that helps kids feel safe in the chair — with sealants and fluoride treatments that protect growing teeth before problems start.

  • Pediatric Dental Care
  • Fluoride Treatment
  • Dental Sealants

Gum Care

Healthy gums are the foundation of a healthy mouth. Regular scaling, deep cleaning, and gum disease treatment stop problems before they affect the teeth themselves.

  • Scaling & Polishing
  • Deep Cleaning
  • Gum Disease Treatment

Meet your dentist

Dr. Muhammad Hanif Khan Niazi

BDS · Founder & Chief Dental Surgeon

Dr. Muhammad Hanif Khan Niazi is a dedicated dental surgeon based in Mianwali, known for his commitment to quality dental care and patient satisfaction. He graduated with a Bachelor of Dental Surgery (BDS) from de'Montmorency College of Dentistry, Lahore, in 2006, and has since worked across both public and private healthcare — gaining extensive experience in preventive, restorative, cosmetic, and surgical dentistry.

He is widely recognized for his compassionate approach, attention to detail, and focus on modern dental treatments — using advanced techniques and modern equipment to keep care comfortable and affordable.

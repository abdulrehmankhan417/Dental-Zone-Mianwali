Our dental services

Comprehensive care for every stage of your smile.

From routine checkups to root canals and oral surgery, Dental Zone Mianwali covers preventive, cosmetic, restorative, and surgical needs — for patients of all ages.

General Dentistry

Your first line of defense against cavities and gum problems. Regular checkups let Dr. Hanif catch issues early — before they turn into pain or expensive procedures.

  • Dental Consultation
  • Oral Examination
  • Preventive Care

Cosmetic Dentistry

For patients who want their smile to look as healthy as it feels. We plan whitening and smile-design cases around your natural face shape, not a one-size template.

  • Teeth Whitening
  • Smile Designing
  • Composite Bonding
  • Cosmetic Fillings

Restorative Dentistry

Rebuilding teeth that have been damaged by decay, wear, or injury — using tooth-colored materials so the repair blends in and function is fully restored.

  • Tooth-Colored Fillings
  • Dental Crowns
  • Dental Bridges
  • Dentures

Root Canal Treatment

When decay or infection reaches the nerve, root canal therapy saves the natural tooth instead of extracting it. Dr. Hanif specializes in comfortable single-visit procedures wherever possible.

  • Single Visit Root Canal
  • Multi Visit Root Canal
  • Tooth Restoration

Oral Surgery

From a straightforward extraction to an impacted wisdom tooth, surgical procedures are performed with sterilized instruments and careful attention to a smooth recovery.

  • Tooth Extraction
  • Wisdom Tooth Removal
  • Minor Oral Surgical Procedures

Children's Dentistry

Gentle, patient care that helps kids feel safe in the chair — with sealants and fluoride treatments that protect growing teeth before problems start.

  • Pediatric Dental Care
  • Fluoride Treatment
  • Dental Sealants

Gum Care

Healthy gums are the foundation of a healthy mouth. Regular scaling, deep cleaning, and gum disease treatment stop problems before they affect the teeth themselves.

  • Scaling & Polishing
  • Deep Cleaning
  • Gum Disease Treatment

Urgent care

Dental emergencies don't wait — neither do we.

Severe toothache, a knocked-out tooth, a broken filling, or facial swelling from infection all need fast attention. Call ahead and we'll make room for you the same day wherever possible.

  • Severe or sudden tooth pain
  • Knocked-out or chipped tooth
  • Lost filling or crown
  • Facial swelling or dental abscess
  • Bleeding that won't stop after an injury
Call for emergency care →
While you wait for help
  • Rinse gently with warm salt water
  • Apply a cold compress to reduce swelling
  • For a knocked-out tooth, keep it moist (milk or saliva) and get to the clinic within 30–60 minutes
  • Avoid very hot, cold, or hard foods until seen

Your safety, guaranteed

Strict sterilization, every instrument, every time.

Patient safety is non-negotiable. Every instrument that touches your mouth follows a documented sterilization process before it's used again.

01

Pre-cleaning

Instruments are cleaned of visible debris immediately after use.

02

Ultrasonic Cleaning

Deep cleaning removes microscopic contaminants from every surface.

03

Autoclave Sterilization

Instruments are sterilized under high-pressure steam to eliminate all pathogens.

04

Sealed Storage

Sterilized tools are sealed and stored until the moment they're needed.

05

Single-Use Disposables

Needles, gloves, and certain items are single-use and disposed of after every patient.
